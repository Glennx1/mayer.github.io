John Mayer

Guitar Virtuoso & Singer-Songwriter

John Clayton Mayer is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist born on October 16, 1977. Known for his intricate guitar work and soulful voice, Mayer has won seven Grammy Awards and has sold over 20 million albums worldwide.
